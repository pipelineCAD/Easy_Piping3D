Easy Piping3D & BOQ

Easy Piping3D & BOQ is a desktop engineering software designed to simplify 3D piping modeling and automatically generate Bill of Quantities (BOQ) and Bill of Materials (BOM) directly inside AutoCAD.

What Easy Piping3D & BOQ Does

  • Design piping systems in 3D inside AutoCAD
  • Convert your routing in 2D polylines into 3D piping with elevation control
  • Insert valves, branches, fittings, and components at any stage
  • Quickly modify or delete piping elements
  • Generate BOQ / BOM and export quantities to Excel
