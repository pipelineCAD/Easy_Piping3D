Easy Piping3D & BOQ

Easy Piping3D & BOQ is a desktop engineering software designed to simplify 3D piping modeling and automatically generate Bill of Quantities (BOQ) and Bill of Materials (BOM) directly inside AutoCAD.

What Easy Piping3D & BOQ Does

  • Design piping systems in 3D inside AutoCAD 2021 and higher
  • Convert 2D polylines into 3D piping with elevation control
  • Insert valves, branches, fittings, and components at any stage
  • Quickly modify or delete piping elements
  • Generate BOQ / BOM and export quantities to Excel

Who It Is For

  • Piping engineers
  • MEP designers
  • Industrial and building project teams
  • Engineering companies and consultants

Key Benefits

  • Fast and intuitive 3D piping modeling
  • Accurate material quantities for budgeting
  • Reduced manual work and errors
  • Works directly in AutoCAD environment
